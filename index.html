    Liturgy

    Join us for our celebration of the Eucharist as we rejoice in our lives as proud Lesbian, Gay, Bisexual, Transgender, Queer and Intersex persons and our friends.

    Every Sunday, 6:30 PM. We are back at the Church with virtual broadcast online each Sunday. Please see our weekly Worship Aid for the meeting id or email OnlinePrayer@DignityNY.org for address or to be added to our weekly distribution list or with any questions concerning our Sunday liturgy.
