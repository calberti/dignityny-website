Liturgy

Dignity/New York celebrates a progressive, inclusive and affirming Eucharistic Liturgy welcoming Lesbian, Gay, Bisexual, Transgender, Queer and Intersex persons and our friends.

Our community is gender-inclusive and gender balanced with diverse presiders. All are invited to participate in communion — the shared Eucharist is an icon of US, for WE are the "Body of Christ."
